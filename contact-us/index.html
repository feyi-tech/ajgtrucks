Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that starting a trucking business can be overwhelming. Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive from aspiring trucking entrepreneurs.

  • How do I apply for the big rigs?
  • How long does it take to get the big rigs?
  • Can I get more than two trucks?
Learn More
What are the eligibility criteria?
To be eligible for the big rigs, you should have a relevant experience in the trucking industry, and demonstrate a commitment to building a successful business.
How do I apply for the big rigs?
To apply for the big rigs, simply click the 'Get Started' button found throughout our site, or select the 'Contact Us' option from the menu. Fill out the brief form, and submit it. One of our dedicated agents will promptly reach out to guide you through the quick and easy process of getting your big rigs and starting your own trucking business. Don’t wait—take the first step toward your dream today!
How long does it take to get the big rigs?
The time it takes to get your big rigs depends on how quickly you apply, as we operate on a first-come, first-served basis. The sooner you start, the sooner you can secure your rigs and begin your trucking business. Don’t miss out—get started today to get ahead in the line!

Our mission is to help truckers start their own businesses by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed. Join us in making the dream of owning a trucking business a reality.
